Interim Championship Summary:

Tony Ferguson weathered the early storm from the young hungry Kevin Lee. After Ferguson threw an inside leg kick, Lee countered with a right hand dropping Tony momentarily. Lee pressures looking for the finish, however Tony catches him with a left lead hook. Lee ends the round going for a takedown, after some scrambling exchanges he ends up on top. Lee found himself in full mount trying to finish the fight with ground and pound. Fortunatly for Tony he defended well and the bell rang indicating the end of the first round. The second round was a striking battle on the feet, however Tonys pace and pressure were visably starting to take a toll on Lee. Lee enters the third round looking to switch it up and utilyzing his wrestling skills. Securing a total of three takedowns in the round, however the third proved to be his last. Ferguson was able to stay aggressive on his back eventually catching Lee in a triangle choke and going on to win the UFC lightweight interim championship belt
